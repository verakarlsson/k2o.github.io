k ² O







k²O (Vera Karlsson, Jan Kunkel, Alie O.) is an artist group at the critical juncture of moving image, text, performance & sound.

Operating under the premise of diluted authorship, k²O bridges sensory gaps between the political, the psychic & the social via
multivalent artistic strategies, such as imitation & substitution.

k²O produces videos, objects, performances & publications alongside complex, context-specific interventions. Their background
as sound & visual artist, performer & writer form the basis for using genre-bending installations as an affirmative medium.
